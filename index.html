Legal Services

LEGAL SERVICE

LEGAL AUDITOR

A Legal Auditor is a professional who has expertise in auditing the legal aspects of an organization or company. The main task of a Legal Auditor is to ensure that the company complies with all applicable laws and regulations, and to identify potential legal risks that may affect the company's operations and reputation.

LEGAL CONTRACT DRAFTER

A legal drafter is a professional who has expertise in drafting and designing legal documents, such as contracts, agreements, powers of attorney, and other legal documents. The main task of a Legal Drafter is to ensure that the legal documents drafted are clear, precise, and in accordance with applicable regulations.

INDUSTRIAL RELATIONS DISPUTE SETTLEMENT LAW

Industrial relations dispute resolution law is a part of labor law that regulates ways of resolving disputes between workers (trade unions) and employers (companies) in the context of employment relationships. These disputes can arise from various issues, such as termination of employment, wages, working conditions, and workers' rights.

TAX LEGAL CONSULTANT

A tax law consultant is a professional who has expertise in tax law and provides tax-related advice and services to individuals, companies, and organizations. The main task of a tax law consultant is to help clients understand and comply with applicable tax regulations, as well as plan efficient tax strategies.

BANKING LEGAL CONSULTANT

A banking legal consultant is a professional who has expertise in the field of law relating to the banking and finance industry. They provide legal advice and services to banks, financial institutions, and other clients involved in financial transactions.
